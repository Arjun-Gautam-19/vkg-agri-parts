About Us

Two decades in exports. Now focused on the field.

VKG Agri Parts is the agricultural division of VKG Impex. For over twenty years, we've been shipping genuine OEM and Tier 1 aftermarket parts out of New Delhi to distributors across Asia, Africa, and Latin America.

We started this division because agricultural buyers needed something different. Harvest doesn't wait. A tractor down in peak season costs a farmer real money. The right part has to fit, last a full season under load, and get there before the work starts. That's a different conversation from selling car parts, and it deserves its own focus.

What We Do

We send tractor and farm equipment parts from India to independent distributors, retailers, and workshops around the world. Engines, hydraulics, filters, clutches, transmissions, electricals, the consumables that get used up every season — that's our range.

Some markets want branded parts. Others do better with unbranded Tier 1 quality at a sharper price. We do both. The part is the same either way; we just match the labelling to what works for you.

Built on Twenty Years of Groundwork

We're not new to this. VKG Impex has spent two decades building the things that actually matter for export — a real supplier network with hundreds of Indian manufacturers, clean documentation, working shipping lanes across Asia, Africa, and Latin America, and most importantly, distributors who keep coming back because the parts showed up when promised and worked the way we said they would.

That's why we can offer what newer exporters struggle with — low minimum orders, flexible payment terms, four to five week lead times, and all the export paperwork handled in-house so you don't have to chase it.

Who We Work With

Independent distributors. Family-run parts shops. Workshop networks. Re-exporters serving rural markets. Anyone who needs reliable parts at a fair landed cost without the markup of going through a regional middleman.

Big or small, the minimums are reasonable and the conversation is straightforward. The fastest way to find out if we can supply what you need is to send us a part number.
